About Me

My name is Ryan Politis. I'm 20 years old and a junior undergraduate computer science major currently attending the University of Massachusetts Lowell. Ever since I was young, I've been fascinated with computers. My first encounter with a computer was with an old desktop in pre-school. The only game on it was SkiFree and I remember taking turns with the other kids and trying to see how far down the mountain each of us could go. Another memorable experience I had with a computer was playing games such as Wizard 101, Club Penguin, and Roblox on my Dad's old desktop. These early interactions with computers inspired me to take an interest in how they work and influenced me to get a degree in computer science.
